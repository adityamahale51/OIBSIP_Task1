Types of Cakes

Chocolate Cake

A rich and indulgent cake made with layers of moist chocolate sponge and decadent chocolate ganache.

Vanilla Cake

A classic cake with a light and fluffy texture, infused with the sweet aroma of vanilla and topped with buttercream frosting.

Red Velvet Cake

A strikingly beautiful cake with a velvety texture and a hint of cocoa, layered with cream cheese frosting.

Carrot Cake

A moist and flavorful cake made with grated carrots, spices, and a cream cheese frosting, perfect for carrot lovers.
